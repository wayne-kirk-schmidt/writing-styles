College Application Template — Collapsible Version

<details>

### 1.1 Personal Introduction

State what you’re applying for and why now.
</details>

---

<details>
  <summary><strong>2. Goals</strong></summary>

### 2.1 Personal Goals  
Your personal growth aims and values.

### 2.2 Educational Goals  
What you wish to study and why.

### 2.3 Career Goals  
Long-term professional direction.
</details>

---

<details>
  <summary><strong>3. Reasons for Application</strong></summary>

### 3.1 Program Fit  
Why this academic program fits your interests.

### 3.2 Institutional Fit  
How the school’s environment and values align with yours.

### 3.3 Personal Alignment  
How your identity, perspective, and experiences align with the institution.
</details>

---

<details>
  <summary><strong>4. Supporting Documents</strong></summary>

### 4.1 Academic Records  
Transcripts, grades, test scores.

### 4.2 Recommendations  
Letters from teachers, mentors, or employers.

### 4.3 Portfolio or Samples  
Work that demonstrates your ability and passion.

### 4.4 Awards & Certifications  
Achievements showing excellence or specialization.
</details>

---

<details>
  <summary><strong>5. Supporting Statements</strong></summary>

### 5.1 Personal Statement Summary  
A high-level summary of your main essay.

### 5.2 Key Experiences  
Critical academic, personal, or leadership experiences.

### 5.3 Skills & Strengths  
Core capabilities and traits you bring.

### 5.4 Additional Context  
Optional background, hardship, or contextual notes.
</details>

---

<details>
  <summary><strong>6. Future Contributions</strong></summary>

### 6.1 Academic Contributions  
How you intend to contribute intellectually.

### 6.2 Community Contributions  
How you will participate in clubs, service, or leadership.

### 6.3 Diversity of Thought & Background  
The unique perspective you bring.
</details>

---

<details>
  <summary><strong>7. Conclusion</strong></summary>

### 7.1 Restatement of Intent  
Reinforce your purpose and direction.

### 7.2 Forward Vision  
How this opportunity fits into your future.

### 7.3 Closing Note  
A confident, positive final statement.
</details>

# c_cover_letter.md  
Cover Letter Template — Collapsible Version

---

<details>
  <summary><strong>1. Greeting</strong></summary>

### 1.1 Recipient Name  
Use the hiring manager’s name when possible to create a respectful, personal connection.

### 1.2 Formal Greeting  
A positive and inclusive greeting such as:  
“Dear Hiring Manager,”  
“Dear Search Committee,”  
“Dear <Name>,”
</details>

---

<details>
  <summary><strong>2. Opening & Purpose</strong></summary>

### 2.1 Position Statement  
State clearly and confidently the role you are applying for.

### 2.2 Hook  
A strong opening line that expresses enthusiasm, purpose, or alignment.

### 2.3 Brief Context  
One sentence explaining why this opportunity resonates with you.
</details>

---

<details>
  <summary><strong>3. Why You</strong></summary>

### 3.1 Core Qualifications  
Highlight your most relevant strengths that align with the role.

### 3.2 Evidence  
Share one or two measurable examples that demonstrate your positive impact.

### 3.3 Alignment to Needs  
Explain how your experience directly supports the team’s goals.
</details>

---

<details>
  <summary><strong>4. Why Them</strong></summary>

### 4.1 Company Fit  
Share what you admire about the organization’s mission, values, or approach.

### 4.2 Role Fit  
Explain why this position is a natural match for your strengths and goals.

### 4.3 Future Intent  
Describe how you aim to contribute positively and collaboratively.
</details>

---

<details>
  <summary><strong>5. Alignment Statement</strong></summary>

### 5.1 Summary of Fit  
A concise, confident paragraph demonstrating why you and the organization align strongly.

### 5.2 Mutual Value  
Emphasize how both you and the team benefit from working together.
</details>

---

<details>
  <summary><strong>6. Closing & Call to Action</strong></summary>

### 6.1 Closing Line  
A positive, appreciative closing sentence.

### 6.2 CTA  
Invite continued conversation:  
“I would welcome the opportunity to discuss…”  
“I look forward to connecting…”

### 6.3 Signature Block  
Your name and professional contact information.
</details>

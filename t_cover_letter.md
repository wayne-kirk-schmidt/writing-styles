# t_cover_letter.md  
Cover Letter Template — Table Version

| Section | Name | Description |
|---------|------|-------------|
| **1** | Greeting | Opens the letter with a respectful, inclusive greeting and establishes tone. |
| 1.1 | Recipient Name | Name of the hiring manager or recruiter, when known. |
| 1.2 | Formal Greeting | Standard openings such as “Dear Hiring Manager,” written positively and respectfully. |
| **2** | Opening & Purpose | Introduces yourself, states your purpose, and sets a confident, forward-focused tone. |
| 2.1 | Position Statement | Identifies the exact role you are applying for. |
| 2.2 | Hook | A strong, positive opening line that engages the reader. |
| 2.3 | Brief Context | One-sentence explanation of why this opportunity matters to you. |
| **3** | Why You | Demonstrates your strengths, experiences, and what you bring to the role with positivity and clarity. |
| 3.1 | Core Qualifications | Key strengths or expertise that align directly with the role. |
| 3.2 | Evidence | One or two concise, measurable examples of your impact. |
| 3.3 | Alignment to Needs | Explains how your background addresses the team’s goals or challenges. |
| **4** | Why Them | Shows respect for the organization, alignment with their values, and enthusiasm for their mission. |
| 4.1 | Company Fit | What draws you to the company’s culture, mission, or work. |
| 4.2 | Role Fit | Why this specific role resonates with your skills and goals. |
| 4.3 | Future Intent | How you plan to make a positive, meaningful contribution. |
| **5** | Alignment Statement | Bridges “Why You” and “Why Them” into a confident, inclusive narrative. |
| 5.1 | Summary of Fit | One paragraph summarizing why you and the organization align. |
| 5.2 | Mutual Value | Expresses how both you and the organization benefit from the partnership. |
| **6** | Closing & Call to Action | Closes with appreciation, confidence, and an open invitation for next steps. |
| 6.1 | Closing Line | Respectful, positive sentence thanking them for their time. |
| 6.2 | CTA | Invitation to continue the conversation or meet. |
| 6.3 | Signature Block | Your name and professional contact information. |

# Research Report Template

## 1. Introduction
- Define the topic.
- Explain context.
- Why it matters.
- Narrow the scope.

## 2. Thesis Statement
- State central question.
- Purpose of analysis.
- Why this topic.

## 3. Quantitative Evidence
- Data, trends, statistics.
- How they support thesis.

## 4. Qualitative Evidence
- Interviews or observations.
- Insights beyond numbers.

## 5. Consequences if Not Addressed
- Physical
- Economic
- Social
- Environmental

## 6. Solutions
### System-Level
- Organizational actions.

### Individual-Level
- Personal or small-group actions.

## 7. Community Action
- Local organizing.
- Collective resilience.

## 8. Conclusion and Call to Action
- Restate thesis.
- Findings.
- Specific actions.

## 9. Reflection
- What was learned.
- Source credibility.
- Bias handling.
- Research challenges.
